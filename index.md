# TechWorkshop L400 on-demand: Case Management Agent

## Where does this fit-in as part of an end-to-end demo?

![image1.png](/media/image1.png)

## Use Case / scenario 
Contoso Coffee is a multinational manufacturer of gourmet coffee and commercial coffee machines. With over 25,000 employees and offices in 18 countries (including the US, Germany, Japan, Brazil, India, and the UK), Their customer service operations are centralized in three global hubs, but support multilingual, multi-channel interactions across time zones.

Throughout your conversations with them, you were able to identify the following pain points:

- High volume of repetitive inquiries such as case status, part availability, and warranty coverage.
- High volume of support cases related to installation, configuration, and maintenance.
- Long resolution times due to fragmented systems and lack of unified customer history.

### Business challenges you are trying to solve

- Reduce average case resolution time and improve first contact resolution.
- Provide a unified case management experience across channels.
- Use AI to assist agents with creation, follow-up and closure.

---

## Learning objectives
- Deploy the necessary prerequisite for the Case Management agent.
- Configure Case Creation with the Case Management agent.
- Configure Case Resolution and Follow-up.
- Demonstrate how Dynamics 365 improves case resolution and agent productivity.

## What you will configure
- Configure tables, forms, and other supporting elements as required.
- Configure mailboxes and app user accounts for agent.
- Enable Copilot and configure Case Management agent capabilities including:

    - Case Creation
    - Case Resolution
    - Case Follow up

## Time to complete 
Estimated time: **90 - 120 minutes**
