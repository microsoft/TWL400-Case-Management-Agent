# Exercise 02: Demonstrate features to a customer


### Talk track

Imagine a customer in Brazil needs to check the status of a service case. Instead of calling the support line, they open the Contoso portal and are greeted by your virtual agent. The agent authenticates the customer, retrieves the case status from Dynamics 365, and provides an update instantly. If the customer needs a technical manual, the agent uses generative AI to locate and summarize the relevant document. For more complex issues, the case is escalated to a live agent, who is assisted by Copilot with contextual knowledge and suggested responses. The Case Management Agent ensures the case is updated and routed appropriately.

### Business value statements

These represent themes that should be part of your narrative. As you demo the functionality to your customer you should come back to these themes and reiterate them throughout you demo.

- Reduce call volumes by up to 40% through intelligent self-service.
- Accelerate agent onboarding by 50% with guided workflows and AI support.
- Improve customer satisfaction with faster, more accurate responses.
- Streamline case resolution with intelligent case management and AI assistance.

### Items to highlight as you're demonstrating

- Emphasize how Copilot reduces agent effort with real-time suggestions.
- Highlight how Case Management Agent automates case creation and classification.
- Show how Knowledge Management turns resolved cases into reusable content.
- Demonstrate multi-session handling for productivity.
